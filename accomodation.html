    • ACCOMODATION

    Distance is no bar.

    Come to Delhi and enjoy your sojourn at Vajra 2015 without worrying about accomodation, we offer a host of various amenities including clean and hygienic rooms, a nutritious and well-balanced diet from the hostel mess and various other facilities needed by attendee sportsmen/women including a badminton court, grounds for practice, and plenty of campus space. Accomodation is available at this point of time, and will be allotted on a first-come first-serve basis. If accomodation is freed up at the last minute, the space created will be given based on factors such as immediate need and date of registration. The decision to give accomodation solely rests with the authorities, and the decision taken by the reqisite authorities will be final and binding. Participants are expected to stay on their best behaviour for the duration of the fest and comply to general hostel guidelines.

    Please do not forget to tick the box in the registration form indicating that you'll be needing accomodation, we might not be able to help you at the last moment if you fail to do so!

    CONVEYANCE

    No longer an annoyance.

    Getting there was never easier. SRM's campus can be reached via NH-8, and is located on the Delhi-Meerut highway. It can be easily reached via public transportation if you use the metro network to reach Vaishali metro station, from where the city buses ("UP Roadways") ply very often. SRM will also run it's own buses along that route for the duration of the event to make it easier for participants to get to the campus and back. Our buses are air-conditioned to help you beat the heat and ride in comfort. Public buses also ply from the ISBT at Kashmere Gate, so you can reach the venue if that is more suitable.
