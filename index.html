    Updates from the team

    Accomodation


    Accomodation will be available at SRM Campus during Vajra 2015, and regular buses will run from Vaishali metro station to the campus as well. College participants will be expected to be on the best of their behaviour during the duration of Vajra 2015 while they stay at SRM quarters. The participants will be provided with good and hygienic accomodation and food for this time period. Click on the blue link labelled "Accomodation" to view more info about accomodation, conveyance, and how to get to SRM University.

    The Organizing Committee


    Behind this fest are a dedicated team of faculty members supported by student co-ordinators, divided into various committees. Click on the link above to view the names of each member, sorted according to committee. Please feel free to contact any student co-ordinator as per your convenience for any assistance you might require for registrations or clarifications regarding the events and competitions.
